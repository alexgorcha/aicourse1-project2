The Design Journey

We designed five distinct visual variations, balancing classic plush toy appeal, modern digital simplicity, and therapeutic presence, before finding Koda's perfect balance.

Stage 1

1. Bedtime Companion

A warm cream and cocoa plush teddy centered purely on sleep routines, ambient noise, and bedtime anxiety relief. Very soft, but slightly too specific.

Stage 2

2. Modern Support Bear

Our first step into an AI modern structure with a sleek oat-white and gray palette, chest heart, and teal display. The structure was perfect, but the colors felt cold.

Stage 3

3. Soft Kawaii Companion

A warm pastel-blush and cream design focused on charm and bright visual positivity. Highly popular with teens, but slightly too juvenile for therapeutic adult support.

Stage 4

4. Therapeutic Plush

An elegant wellness concept boasting high clinical credibility and minimalist design for sensory relief. Felt premium, but missed the traditional teddy warmth.

Final Direction

5. Classic Brown (Chosen)

Our final selected model. Integrates the modern face-screen bezel and paw buttons of Concept 2 into a classic, comforting honey-brown plush body that adults and kids love.

Common Inquiries

Frequently Asked Questions

Have questions about how Koda's AI operates or how safety is handled? We have answers.

How does Koda's AI memory operate?

Koda's conversational history is securely encrypted. By default, Koda stores your companion data locally on its built-in solid-state module. If you opt-in, Koda can securely sync companion memory to your personal cloud so it remains consistent across multiple sizes or device replacements, giving you 100% control to view or delete data at any time.

Is Koda safe for children and toddler use?

Yes! While Koda is extremely popular with young women and sensitive adults, Koda meets all stringent CPSC age-determination and ASTM F963 toy safety regulations. An optional parent-control app is available to lock child profiles, filter conversations, and enforce strictly offline, local-only support modes.

Does Koda require constant Wi-Fi connectivity?

No. Koda has a local offline-processor that handles breathing cues, basic bedtime soundscapes, visual screens, and a small library of supportive comforting remarks. Advanced conversational features and weather/security reminders require Wi-Fi connectivity.

What sizes are available for pre-order?

We are launching with two premium physical sizes:
1. Bedside Companion (12 inches): The perfect size for sitting on your nightstand, desk, or holding securely during stressful moments (default).
2. Travel Companion (8 inches): A lighter, compact size equipped with an integrated travel strap—perfect for carrying inside backpacks and taking on long flights or trips.

Can I wash Koda's plush teddy cover?

Yes! Koda is engineered with a modular zip-open back. Simply slide open the hidden zipper, slip out the cushioned technical processor core and batteries, and wash the soft fabric teddy cover in a standard washing machine (gentle cycle, cold water). Lay flat to dry, then slide the core back in.

How does the weighted sensory core support anxiety?

Koda features an integrated weighted lower core containing hypoallergenic glass micro-beads. This simulates Deep Pressure Touch (DPT)—the same clinical mechanism utilized in weighted blankets and deep-pressure therapy. The weighted pressure triggers the release of serotonin and dopamine while reducing cortisol (stress hormones), aiding physical calm during panic moments or before sleep.
